Welcome to Twin Peaks.

The owls in this town are not what they seem... grab a friend and play as Agent Dale Cooper and The Man from Another Place as you attempt to make matching pairs of owls. Simply click on 1 image and then click on a second image. If these two owls are a match then you get a point and go again. If you don't make a match, the owls will fade away and the other player will then take a turn. The person who gets the most points wins.

This game is lovingly modeled after the early 90s tv program, Twin Peaks.
